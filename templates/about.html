About Us

Focused on supporting agriculture by using deep learning to detect and classify tomato leaf diseases, helping farmers take timely action and improve crop health.

Objective

This mission is to empower farmers, agricultural experts, and researchers with reliable tools to detect and understand tomato leaf diseases early. We aim to support sustainable farming practices by providing accurate, accessible, and user-friendly disease classification tools that promote healthy crop management and reduce agricultural losses.

Technology
