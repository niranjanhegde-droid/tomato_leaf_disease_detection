Tomato Leaf Disease Remedies

Healthy Tomato Leaf

Description: Bright green, smooth-edged leaves with no discoloration, spots, or curling. Healthy leaves indicate balanced watering, proper sunlight, and disease-free conditions.

Tips to Maintain Health:

  • Water at the base to avoid wetting leaves.
  • Use mulch to retain moisture and reduce splash-borne diseases.
  • Inspect regularly for pests or early signs of disease.
  • Provide full sun and adequate spacing between plants.
